### What is PL-400?

The Microsoft Power Platform Developer (PL-400) certification validates your ability to design, develop, secure, and extend Power Platform solutions. This includes creating technical designs, configuring Dataverse, building canvas and model-driven apps, implementing automation with Power Automate, and extending the platform with custom code.

## üíæ Data Storage & Privacy

### Local Storage
- All your progress is stored in your browser's localStorage
- No data is sent to any server
- Your progress persists between sessions (as long as you use the same browser)
- Clearing your browser data will delete your progress

### What Gets Saved
- Completed lesson IDs
- Quiz results (scores, answers, timestamps)
- Activity history (lesson completions, quiz attempts)

### Exporting Your Data
You can export your progress at any time:
- **JSON format**: Complete data structure with all details
- **CSV format**: Simplified tabular format for spreadsheet analysis

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Quiz Not Submitting
- Make sure you've answered all 10 questions
- Check that JavaScript is enabled in your browser

### Progress Not Saving
- Ensure cookies and localStorage are enabled
- Check that you're not in private/incognito mode (progress won't persist)
- Some browsers have strict privacy settings that may block localStorage

### Export Not Working
- Check your browser's download settings
- Ensure pop-ups are not blocked
- Try a different browser if issues persist
